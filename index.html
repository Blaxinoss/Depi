Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation online, by phone, or in person at one of our locations.

What do I need to bring with me when I pick up the car?

You will need to bring a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a credit card for the deposit.

How much is the fuel charge?

You are responsible for returning the vehicle with the same fuel level as when you picked it up. If the fuel level is lower, you will be charged a refueling fee.

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the reservation type and time of cancellation. Please refer to our terms and conditions for more details.
